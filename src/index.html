About Dewi

Passionate about origami for more than 10 years and more recently textile pleating, Dewi Brunet has developed strong skills and knowledge of folding. He exhibits his work, organizes workshops, creates interior design, sell objects and contributes to events. He is always eager to teach and share the multiple bridges between folding, science, architecture, technology and fashion. You can take direct contact with him for any project or idea you would like to implement, or visit his Instagram or Facebook page for an overview of his work.

Latest projects

ReACT by Design

ReACT by Design is an international design workshop week at the Faculty of Architecture of Antwerpen. For the 2018 edition, Dewi Brunet collaborated with architect Elsa Bouillot to propose a workshop on nomad design. Students were invited to create a large folded structure out of urban wastes. Artisan 2.0

Artisan 2.0 was a series of workshops organized with the fablab Openfab. The aim of this applied research open to the public was to show how new technologies could blend with traditional techniques like folding. After 10 days of workshop, a series of prototype were designed, including a self-sufficient biomimetic lamp able to catch the sun during the day and retract at night to light up.
